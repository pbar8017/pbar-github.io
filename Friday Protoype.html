Task 6: Description of Low-Fidelity Prototype Aligned with Value Proposition

There are 3 main sections of the App designed on the low-fidelity prototype: Goals Section, EduTech Section, and the Social Section.

The prototype visualizes the steps that users take to create and manage goals, access educational resources, and engage with a supportive community. The detailed description and images below illustrate the user experience and functionality of each section.

Detailed Descriptions

Goals Section

The Goals section helps users define, plan, and manage their life goals effectively. Templates and guided tools ensure the goal-setting process is simple and engaging.

EduTech Section

The EduTech section offers educational resources, including articles, podcasts, and master classes, to help users achieve their goals with expert guidance.

Social Section

The Social section fosters a sense of community by allowing users to share their goals and achievements while gaining inspiration and support from others.
