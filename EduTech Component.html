Introduction

The EduTech component of the Friday app is designed to empower users through educational insights derived from real-world data. This report highlights key trends and findings based on a comprehensive dataset, aiming to foster better decision-making and inspire growth in various life domains.

Understanding Demotivation: Key Barriers

Based on user feedback, several recurring themes were identified as barriers to achieving personal and professional goals.

Qualitative Insights

  • Time Constraints: Struggles with balancing responsibilities and goal-setting.
  • Lack of Energy: Feeling too tired to take action on goals.
  • Unclear Goals: Difficulty defining actionable objectives.
  • External Obstacles: Challenges like bureaucracy and societal expectations.
  • Self-Doubt: Low confidence and fear of failure.
  • Monotony and Boredom: Loss of interest in repetitive tasks.

Key Trends and Insights

Fitness Goals

Users aim to build routines that enhance physical health and consistency. Accountability systems are seen as vital to success.

Career Aspirations

Focus is on achieving long-term career milestones with planning and perseverance, though external factors pose challenges.

Skill Development

Many seek to gain new creative or technical skills, often hindered by time constraints or a lack of resources.

Personal Growth

Goals centered around self-improvement, including better habits and mental health practices, dominate this category.

Conclusion

The insights from this report highlight the diverse needs and aspirations of users. By addressing these trends, the EduTech component can deliver personalized resources and tools to empower users on their journeys toward achieving their goals.
